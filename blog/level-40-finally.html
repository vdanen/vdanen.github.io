Level 40… finally

Well, I’ve been playing WoW for a while now… since the summer of last year. I finally managed to get my druid up to level 40. Yeah, you young guys with nothing but homework are laughing at me now since it’s taken me so long to hit, but whatever… considering how little time I actually have to play, I think I’m doing pretty good.

According to my played time, I’ve spent 5 days, 23hrs, 59mins, and 34secs to go from level 1 to level 40. If I was a single guy with no hobbies, I probably could have done that within a month. So it’s taken me about 6 months (the /played macro doesn’t tell me when I actually started, so I’d have to look at credit card statements to find out when I actually started). So, now I can get a mount! Yippee! Unfortunately it costs about 90-100 gold pieces to pay for the mount and training, and right now I have 45g. So maybe I’ll actually get my mount when I hit 42. =)
