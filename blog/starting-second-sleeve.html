Starting second sleeve

So this past Wednesday I started on finishing up the second sleeve (my right arm). Unfortunately, with my wife’s new business, she’s been too busy to take pictures and I’m not one of these “take pictures of yourself in the mirror” guys. So I’m going to shamelessly link to my artist’s instagr.am picture of it. It’s not the whole tattoo, but it’s a pretty cool looking picture nonetheless.

That’s just the inside of my arm. We went all the way around it and up a little too close to the armpit for my liking. Feels ok now though. =) I’d have taken pictures today, but I’m starting to peel (go reptilian as my daughter calls it), so will have to wait another week to get full pics of the whole thing. Going to try to get this arm finished off this year I think.
