Marriage

This is a list of marriage content from NAME (National Association of Marriage Enhancement) Canada. NAME no longer functions as an entity, yet this material is Biblical and timeless and we use it for marriage counseling at Christcity Church.

The material here is being made available so it can help as many people as possible, the way that we believe both God and Pastor Leo intended.

Apologies for the quality, some of the recordings are quite old and don’t sound amazing but the content is outstanding. Each is available in MP3 format.

Preparing for the Covenant of Marriage

This is the premarital content we use for premarital counseling. Link

I’ve Got You Covered

A major cause of trouble in relationships is when one or both spouses feel insecure. Security leads to trust and intimacy. That’s why it’s so important to “cover” each other, the way a warm comforter covers us on a cold winter morning. These eight powerful and dynamic and live changing messages reveal God’s truth about the concept of “Covering.” Link

True Trust

A surprising and powerfully profound teaching on developing properly placed trust will help every marriage. Significant understanding of how trust breaks down and what it takes to rebuild trust in relationships is a vital part of healthy, fear-free intimacy in marriage. Turning trust breakers into trust builders empowers any spouse to take what could be traumas and utilize them to increase confidence and reliance on God to see couples grow through their trials. This teaching series has been life changing for couples who have gone through long periods of broken trust, while also helping newlyweds develop the means to avoid patterns of distrust. Link

How to Have a Wonderful Spouse

Strength Based Marriages: Marriages are not destroyed by differences but by seflish responses to differences. Learn how to appreciate, be thankful for, and enjoy these differences.

Fear Factor or Faith Factor: Be more than a conqueror, most than an overcomer in your marriage. Learn how to stop wandering in the wilderness of “what-ifs” and see the strength, the glory, and the opportunity in situations.

Wired for Strength: Each person is wired by God with specific strengths and His specific purpose. Learn how to be comrades in a mission rather than combatants.

Chooser or Muser?: Accepting each other as unique; unique in approach and uniquely utilized by God for His purpose. Link

Spiritual Warfare for Every Marriage

The principles of spiritual warfare applied to the marital relationship fend off the enemy in this area of desperate need. Learn how to walk in victory and defeat the devourer in these six sessions. Link

Anger Management for Couples

Be angry and sin not.

A totally unique and most effective approach to the challenge of anger in relationships. Anger is a natural emotion. It’s our response to anger that can be destructive or constructive.

Learn how anger comes from the divine nature of God and why some couples experience anger problems together when they don’t have them individually. This pack will also help individuals who struggle with anger learn how to make anger a positive force in their lives.

This teaching has helped many couples experience a breakthrough when nothing else has helped. A four part series, it can be used with the matching couples workbook. Link

Walk in Power

Jesus walked in such power that at the end He could say, “It is finished;” He earned the description of “Faithful and True.” That same power is ours, if we are willing. Link

From Natural to Supernatural

What is keeping you from moving into the supernatural in your life? Self-afflicted boundaries? Self-placed hurdles? We all need to develop intimacy with God by discerning His voice while diminishing the voice of the enemy. Link

Great Shall Be Your Peace

Do you seek peace in your life? Do you have only some peace and only in certain circumstance? Are you looking for peace that enables you to live a life of distinction? Link

Living Stress Fear

Most couples today are stressed out – out of time, out of money, out of energy, out of options – simply put – they are overloaded. Learn how to tap into the power of reserve, understanding how to always have a little left over. This teaching will give you powerful keys to reducing stress and increasing your reserves instead of being depleted, you’ll feel completed! Link

Bold Strong Marriage

Boldness and gentleness can go together, and any couple can learn how to use stress in marriage to develop strengths that they didn’t even know they could have. Learn how to build a blessed relationship for the long run, and in the long run, you will be blessed, and the blessings of God will surprise you and may even overtake you! Link

Sex is War!

This is one of Pastor Leo’s most renowned audio sets. Churches all over the nation have gathered couples to hear what has been called “the most graphic teaching on sexual issues ever in a church setting.” This anointed and upfront teaching is a must for every married couple to understand sexuality! Link

Topics Not Touched in Church

NOTE: Adult topics

This series goes into detail and boldly faces topics many are afraid to touch: including lust. It fills a need to examine scripture concerning issues that face today’s church but are often ignored. This is a learning tool that will help strengthen your mind and spirit. Link

Motivational Gifts in Your Marriage

An in-depth eye-opening, heart-revealing study in the motivational gifts listed in Romans 12 and how they pertain to you and your marriage. Discover what motivates your spouse and how God wired you to motivate each other. Learn which motivational gifts both you and your spouse are predisposed to as primary and secondary gifts and how they affect your purpose and productivity in life and marriage. A unique teaching draws on the parallel structure of the original Greek language to show which attitudes are related to each gift. Many call this a truly life-changing series. Link

Living With Trials

A few months after the sudden passing of her husband, Molly Godzich produced this series. It is an inspiring blend of personal experience with the Biblical principles of trials and tribulations from the book of James. We are all bound to experience despair and heartache during our lifetime. Molly’s timely message may be what you need to hear right now. Will you wait on God in faith until the darkness gives way to light? Will you endure the hardship to gain the wisdom and rewards that God has around the corner? This four-part series will challenge you! Link

The Love Attitude

The attitude that makes the difference. This three part series is designed to help you develop and grow the Love Attitude in your marriage.

A Better Covenant: Learn how to develop the Love Attitude by understanding your marriage as a Better Covenant, not just a contract. Coming to an understanding of what your marriage vows really mean can take you both towards a new attitude: the Love Attitude.

Put Off By Put Ons: In the world today there is a new and incorrect standard: “”who ever is slicker, wins!”” Unfortunately many people today have extended this wrong-headed notion to their marriage relationship. Too many people are “”putting on”” their spouses in an attempt to manipulate. What we need to be “”putting on”” is the Love Attitude!

Understanding Compassion: A cousin to Mercy and Empathy, Compassion is different in an important way: Compassion moves you to action. Learn to understand compassion and you will start to see actions in your own life and marriage: actions that can make the difference. Link

Marriage is a Process

Most couples spend more time, energy, and resources planning for their wedding than they do for their marriage. Problems surface when couples look at life as a series of events, rather than as a process. Jesus clearly calls for our marriage mantality to be counter-culture. Discover the joy of embracing process and avoiding the emotional rollercoaster of event-based living. Link

Mastering Breakthroughs

Most people know they need breakthroughs in their life, but they don’t know what to do when they get one. This eye-and-heart-opening series unlocks the scriptural keys to not only living with breakthroughs but developing the mastery of going from breakthrough to breakthrough as God accelerates vision and faith in your life. Learn why David named a previously unnamed mountain (read “obstacle”) The Lord Who Breaks Through. This teaching outlines the principles God sets forth for breaking through obstacles, enemies and hindrances to our goal of ever-increasing faith. Link

Rebellion vs Obedience

The Rebellious Marriage: Learn if you and your spouse are walking in obedience with regard to your marriage or if you are in rebellion. This message will challenge you to seek God’s plan for your relationship.

I am not a Doormat: How do you treat your spouse? Do you feel you are better than your spouse? Learn how to build up a healthy environment in your home.

Juggling or Balancing: Do you feel out of control or hectic with how you are managing the details of life? This message will help you learn to balance your priorities.

Taming Turmoil: Learn how to overcome feeling down. Most Christians don’t want to admit that they can feel depressed but it is a reality. This teaching will inspire you to not dwell on those moments but to move on. Link

Your Vow is for Now

Your marriage vows weren’t just for your wedding, they apply today. Your Vow is for Now! This two-part series will enlighten you to the true meaning of your marriage vows and the importance of a kept vow. In a time when people break their word and their vows easily, thinking there are no consequences, you will learn how vitally important vows really are. Link

The Tent of Together

From the Tabernacle of the wilderness to the Tent of Meeting to a tent “not made with human hands,” the imagery of the tent as a place to meet with God and each other has profound implications for experiencing the presence of God in your life and marriage. You’ll be enthralled by God’s majesty and glory as you glean from these teachings that will help you behold His glory in your life and marriage. You will learn to expect and experience a glorious change of scenery each time you enter and leave the tent! Link

Self Image in the Spirit

In a world that emphasizes self esteem learn that it is Self-Image that is Scriptural. A poor self-image can make you miss God. A proper image of who you are in God will stir up gifts and anointing. Learn to see yourself as God sees you. Link

What God Requires

In our world today all sorts of advice is given to married couples. But what does God require? These six messages will show you three forgotten values that God requires in all of our relationships. Link

Love When it Hurts

There are many people in the world today who have loved and lost in love. The pains are deep seated and tend to linger — so much, that we are afraid to try to love again. This four part series will help you overcome your hurts. Learn to become all God wants you to be. Link

Love Positions

Many were blessed and had their eyes opened to what God says about our positions together in marriage. You’ll be edified and pleasantly surprised and informed as you get in the right position to be blessed. Warning: “Laying Together” contains graphic (but not explicit) material for mature, married audiences only. Link

Pure in Heart

Included in the Beatitudes, Christ says that the pure in heart will see God. In this teaching series, we learn how to purify and posture our hearts towards God and our spouse. The following teachings deal with issues of the heart that we all could grow in. Pastor Leo’s timeless teachings continue to inspire us all to watch and guard our hearts. Link

The Truth About Love

This six part series brings new perspective to the meaning of love. You’ll be inspired by Pastor Leo’s insights as they are explained with the framework of a Biblical format. This set will help you see ways to react and things you can change to build a happier, healthier relationship. Link

Jezebel in America

The mighty prophet Elijah was so afraid of the evil Queen Jezebel that he ran and hid and asked God to take his life! Learn how the Spirit of Jezebel lives in our nation and churches today; a “must listen” if you want to understand our times. Link

Listen Love

This much needed, popular series has three messages on one of the most forgotten, vital parts of communication in marriage — the art of listening. Link

As Serious as Cyrus

Cyrus was anointed and appointed by God to receive supernatural blessings in a more extreme way than others. Learn how God can use you to further his purposes as you realize the blessings God has for you. Link

Distracted No More

This three teaching series by Molly Godzich identies one’s propensity to distractions, showing how distractions move into our lives to keep us from focusing on God’s best in our lives and families. Learn to safeguard your time and life and what common distractions plague us all. Molly then provides practical points on how to continually refocus and reprioritize so that you can maximize God’s benefits and favor in your life. Link

Climate Control

This three part study will help you measure, adjust and set the temperature of your home so that your family will not only survive, but thrive. Based on scripture from the Psalms concerning marriage and family, this set will encourage you to develop a Godly and fruitful home. Link

Confidence Trust Courage

Developing the personal freedom to succeed in your walk with God.

Worry No More!: This message gives you the Scriptural keys to overcoming worry. Learn to live without tormenting or disturbing thoughts. Practical advice for worry-free living.

Developing Trust & Confidence: Do you know that the verse in the exact middle of the Bible presents God’s central theme for living a bold and free life? Learn to place your focus and trust on God and develop the confidence to achieve His purposes in your life.

H.O.W. to Have Courage: Learn H.O.W. to have courage, an easy to remember way to overcome discouragement. Listen and grow to encourage yourself in the Lord. Link

Help me Deal with my Spouse, Lord!

Knowing your spouse’s needs, understanding his or her emptional capacity and dealing with our annoying habits are all important parts of a great marriage. These three messages are presented in a humorous manner that will help you deal with your spouse (and your spouse to deal with you!) Link
